Hello!

My name's Andrea, and I'm a graphic designer from Kokomo, Indiana. Just this May, I graduated from Indiana University with a B.S. in New Media and web design concentration. Although I'm no longer a student, I'm willing to learn new things and hone my skills for many years to come.

My goal is to make clean, harmonious designs that communicate messages in a unique way. I enjoy experimenting with typography, color, and illustrative elements the most. I also do photo retouching and manipulation, as well as web design.

The programs I'm most familiar with are the Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. For web design, I have extensive experience with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, and jQuery.
