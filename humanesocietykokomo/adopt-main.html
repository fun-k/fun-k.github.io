Adopt

We welcome you to come and visit the shelter to spend time and hopefully adopt a new friend for life. Although we try hard to, we are not always able to post all of the available animals. You may view their photos and profiles online at Petfinder and Petango.

To speed up the adoption process you can download our adoption application.

Please present your completed application in person or fax it to (765) 454-0082. Mailed applications are not accepted.

Holding Services

We welcome your interest in our animals, and it is our policy to offer "hold service" when possible. Animals can be held for 24 hours with a $25 non-refundable deposit which is applicable towards the adoption fee. After 48 hours, the animal will be placed back up for adoption.

If you are traveling in the hopes of adopting one of our pets, please call ahead to ensure the animal you wish to adopt has not already been adopted.

If you have questions feel free to email us at adoption@kokomohumane.org.

Canine Adoption Fees

Dogs under six months: $130
Dogs over six months: $120
Previously altered Dogs: $50

Feline Adoption Fees

We have reduced our feline adoption fees for the the time being to the fees listed below.

Cats and kittens: $80
Previously altered cats: $30

Included in your adoption fee

  • Spay/neuter surgery. (you will be responsible for additional vaccines and services)
  • Vaccinations received at shelter
  • Microchip and registration
  • 30-day gift of pet health insurance
  • 1 or 2 pound bag of Hill's Science Diet pet food
  • Leash (dogs)
  • Training Your Adopted Dog DVD (dogs)
  • Your Adopted Cat DVD (cats)
  • Cardboard cat box
  • Rabies certificate provided for dogs adopted at $120 or $130

And the most important part...

You will receive the unconditional love of a wonderful animal AND a second chance for the adopted animal.

Adoption fees are donations to the Kokomo Humane Society, and are refundable only with the approval of the Executive Director.

Please note that certain animals have special fees associated with them. We accept cash or credit only for the adoption fee. No checks allowed.
